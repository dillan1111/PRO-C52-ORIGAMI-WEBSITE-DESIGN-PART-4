Interesting Facts about Camels

  1. The ability of the camels to stay for weeks without having to drink water led to them being nicknamed “ships of the desert"

  2. They store fat in parts of their bodies known as humps

  3. Camel milk is among the healthiest milk in the world

Interesting Facts about Pandas

  • Despite being herbivores, pandas evolved to have a shorter gut, which is usually only seen in carnivores.

  • Wild pandas may all be herbivores, but it’s a different story for captive pandas. In zoos and sanctuaries, pandas have a more varied diet of eggs, fish, and produce.

  • Baby pandas are born pink.

Interesting Facts about Chameleon

  • Oldest Identified Lived 60 Million Years Ago. As far as paleontologists can tell, the first chameleons evolved shortly after the extinction of dinosaurs 65 million years ago.

  • Over 200 Species. Classified as "old world" lizards because they're only indigenous to Africa and Eurasia, chameleons consist of a dozen named genera and over 200 individual species.

  • "Chameleon" Means "Ground Lion"
